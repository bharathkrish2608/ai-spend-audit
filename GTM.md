# GTM.md

## Exact Target User

Not "startups." The specific person is an **engineering manager or CTO at a 3-15 person startup that raised a seed or Series A in the last 18 months.**

They have:
- At least 3 engineers on the team
- A monthly AI tools bill somewhere between $200-$2000
- No dedicated finance person watching SaaS spend
- Signed up for tools individually during the chaos of early growth and never audited them

Their specific pain: they know they're probably overpaying but it's never urgent enough to sit down and figure out. The bill just gets paid every month.

Job titles: CTO, VP Engineering, Engineering Manager, Technical Co‑founder.

Company stage: post‑seed, pre‑Series B. Usually 10‑50 employees total.

## What They Google Before Wanting This Tool

- "cursor vs github copilot 2026"

- "chatgpt team vs enterprise pricing"
- "how much does ai tooling cost per developer"

These are comparison and pricing queries — people already thinking about cost, not people who just discovered AI tools.
